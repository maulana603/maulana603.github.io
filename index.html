Biography


Maulana is a final-year master's student studying Modelling and Molecular Simulation at BIOPHAM EMJMD program. He has been working with Prof. Takahashi Satoshi and Kiyoto Kamagata as a researcher in liquid-liquid phase separation proteins at the Biological Molecular Dynamics Laboratory. He is now mainly studying simulation algorithm, molecular modelling and simulation in his master.


Maulana is an avid and curious student. He has strong background in chemistry and material science, but his skill is not limited to wet laboratory techniques. He also has programming skills that he obtained through research projects, classes, and self-study. He is recently interested in the data science and molecular simulation. He finished the training program of data scientist from Ministry of Communication of Indonesia. He is also constantly improving his skills and knowledge by taking online course in LeetCode and Udemy.


He can speak Japanese, English and Indonesian. While free, Maulana enjoys to play bdaminton and travelling.
